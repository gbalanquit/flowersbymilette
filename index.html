ABOUT ME

Milette has been decorating weddings and events for over a decade. Her designs are inspiring, creative and outright genius. The ability to create a color plan within the floral arrangements, with you, is essential to every aspect of your wedding. Her creative genius allows you flexibility in your budget. Flowers are essential for all aspects of any wedding event, she is sure to use the flowers of the season to cut your costs. Her ideas of floral placement in the wedding and dual duty (reuse) from the wedding to the reception brings your budget to affordability. Her vision during your planning stages will add elegance and whimsy to your amazing day. Milette can see beyond your vision and bring to you a fabulous romantic moment with the one you love.
